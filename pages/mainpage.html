before you begin

we are not just a study tool.
we are your learning companion.

It started during finals week, with two overwhelmed students and a shared Google Doc. We weren't trying to build the next big ed-tech platform — we were trying to create something that actually made studying feel less lonely. Something that felt like having a friend next to you, even at 2 AM with a cold coffee.

Study Buddy was born from those late-night study sessions where we realized that the best learning happens when you have someone in your corner just a calm, supportive space that helps you stay focused and motivated.

"We believe studying shouldn't feel like a chore — it should feel like growth, with a friend by your side."

Who we are: A small team of students, friends, for better learning environments. We've been through the all-nighters, the confusion, the frustration of not knowing where to start. So we built Study Buddy to be the companion we wish we had back then — organized, encouraging, and always there when you need it.

Why this website? Because we wanted to create a space where learning feels human again. The actual site holds all the tools, resources, and features to help you and also entertain you, but before you dive in, we wanted you to know why we built this with so much care. This is our story. The rest is your journey.
