Freshman Guide

Welcome 🎓

Starting college can feel overwhelming, but everyone is new like you. Take your time, explore, and remember that you belong here.
Go to Class 📚

Attending lectures regularly keeps you on track. Even when you feel lazy, showing up makes a big difference.
Make Friends 🤝

Talk to classmates and build connections. Friends make studying easier and college life more enjoyable.
Ask for Help ❓

If something is unclear, ask early. Professors and classmates are there to help you succeed.
Take Care ❤️

Sleep well, eat well, and rest. Your health is more important than any grade.
