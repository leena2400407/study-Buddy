1. General Overview

Established in 1996, MIU is one of Egypt’s leading private universities. It was founded with the vision of providing a modern, high-quality education that bridges the gap between Egyptian traditions and international academic standards.

Location: KM 28 Cairo – Ismailia Road, Ahmed Orabi District, Cairo, Egypt.

2. Academic Hubs

  • Faculty of Al-Alsun (Applied Linguistics): Focused on languages and translation.
  • Faculty of Business Administration & International Trade: Covering Finance, Marketing, and Management.
  • Faculty of Computer Science: Specializing in Software Engineering and Information Technology.
  • Faculty of Engineering: Including Architecture and Electronics & Communications.
  • Faculty of Mass Communication: Radio & TV, Journalism, and Integrated Marketing Communication (IMC).
  • Faculty of Oral & Dental Medicine: Known for its state-of-the-art clinics.
  • Faculty of Pharmacy: Offering both Pharm D and Clinical Pharmacy programs.

3. Why MIU?

  • Global Partnerships: MIU maintains active exchange programs and academic collaborations with renowned universities in the US and Europe.
  • Accreditation: Programs are recognized by the Egyptian Ministry of Higher Education and often carry international certifications in their respective fields.
  • Career Preparation: The university hosts an Annual Employment Fair and provides career counseling to help students transition into professional roles.
  • Modern Infrastructure: The campus features advanced labs, a comprehensive e-learning system, and specialized facilities like the dental clinics and media studios.

4. Student Life & Culture

A university isn't just about books. MIU is famous for its vibrant campus atmosphere:

  • Student Clubs: From Tuners (Music) and ASCC (Charity) to the Gamers Lounge and MIU Magazine (3alwara2).
  • Annual Events: Highlights include International Day (celebrating global cultures) and Sports Day.
  • Campus Vibes: Known for its green spaces and "The Plaza," which serves as the social heart of the university.
